Research Interest

With behavioral experiments, EEG, fNIRS, eyetracking and social media data, I study a wide range of research questions in Social Cognitive Neuroscience, including:
  • Implicit attitude, social learning and memory: see Chen et al. Poster Presented in SANS 2022
  • Sleep and memory: see Xia et al., bioRxiv
  • Face Perception and social characteriestics: see Zheng et al., 2022
  • Prosocial behavior and emotion and emotional memory: see Varma#, Chen# et al., 2022
  • Hyperscanning: see Chen#, Zhang# et al., 2022
  • Data visualization
